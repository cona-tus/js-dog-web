We would like to help you.

  • How does the Doggy service operate?

    Doggy opefee through a dedicated app, allowing owners to schedule services such as walks, customized meals, and waste removal at desired times and frequencies. Owners can monitor Doggy's activities in real-time through the app.

  • How does Doggy handle special dietary requirements for my dog?

    Doggy considers any special dietary requirements provided by the owner, offering personalized meals based on the dog's health status and dietary preferences. Owners can set health conditions and meal preferences on the app for optimal meal suggestions.

  • What does Doggy provide to the dog during walks?

    During walks, Doggy not only ensures a safe walking experience but also provides water and snacks afterward to help the dog recover.

  • How does Doggy manage the health of the dog?

    Doggy collects data on the dog's activity levels, food consumption, and bathroom habits. It regularly provides owners with health reports based on this data. If any issues are detected, the owner is promptly notified.

  • What should I do if issues arise during the use of Doggy's services?

    Doggy offers 24/7 customer support. Owners can report problems within the app or contact customer service via phone or email. Our team is ready to provide assistance promptly.
